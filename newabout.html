About Us

Welcome to Foodie!

We'reFOODIE and this is the ultimate platform for food lovers who are too busy to whip up elaborate meals!

Here, we have a collection of recipes submitted by our users from all around the world. They are quick and simple and you can whip up dishes from all over the world in under an hour!

As fellow busy university students, we love eating home cooked food because we miss food from home; but yet we have very little time to search for recipes from various different sites. Thus, we came up with this; a one stop destination for all the food you crave. All recipes come with step by step instructions and cooking times so you can plan your meals properly!
